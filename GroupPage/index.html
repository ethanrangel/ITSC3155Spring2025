Our Mission Statement

We are a team of passionate students from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte, each bringing unique strengths in cloud engineering, financial analysis, and software development. Our mission is to grow together through hands-on collaboration, leveraging our diverse backgrounds to solve real-world challenges with innovation and integrity.

We believe in learning by doing. Through group projects, internships, and coursework, we continuously push ourselves to stay ahead of the curve in a fast-changing tech landscape. As future professionals, we’re committed to using our skills not only to build high-impact solutions, but also to foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels empowered to contribute.

Our shared vision is to build a lasting impact, both within the UNC Charlotte community and beyond, by creating technology that is ethical, efficient, and transformative. Together, we are IEI: Intelligent, Evolving, and Inspired.
